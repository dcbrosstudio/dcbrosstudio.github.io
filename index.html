Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my progress?

Sign in with your Google or Apple account to sync your progress across devices. Your scores and completed puzzles will be automatically saved to your account.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings in the app, scroll down, and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your data from our servers.

Why am I seeing ads?

Ads help us keep the game free for everyone. You can watch rewarded ads to earn hints and other bonuses!

How do leaderboards work?

Complete daily challenges to compete on global and regional leaderboards. Your best scores are saved and ranked against other players.

The app is crashing or not working properly

Try closing and reopening the app, or reinstalling it. Make sure you have the latest version installed. If the problem persists, please contact us with details about your device and the issue.
