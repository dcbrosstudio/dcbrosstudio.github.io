Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 6, 2025

Summary: Freebies collects minimal data necessary to provide app functionality. We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Freebies: Birthday, Credit Card ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Freebies, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive:

  • Email address - Used for account identification
  • Display name - Shown in your profile (you can change this)
  • Profile photo URL - From your OAuth provider

When you create an account with email and password:

  • Email address - Used for account identification and verification
  • Password - Securely managed by Firebase Authentication (we never store your password directly)
  • Username - Provided at account creation and shown in your profile

If you use the app as a guest, we do not collect any personal information until you choose to sign in.

2.2 User-Provided Data

  • Birthday dates and names - Birthdays you add to track perks
  • Event subscriptions - Birthday events you choose to follow
  • Credit card selections - Cards you track for perks (we do not collect actual card numbers)
  • Votes - Your thumbs up/down on community events

2.3 Usage Data

  • Perk usage tracking - When you mark perks as used (stored locally on your device)
  • Event completions - Tracking which birthday perks you've redeemed

2.4 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Credit card numbers or payment information
  • Location data
  • Contacts or phone data
  • Biometric data
  • Browsing history

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Sync your data across devices when signed in
  • Display community-created birthday events
  • Allow you to track your perk usage
  • Improve the app experience

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

Data Type Storage Location
Account data, subscriptions, votes Google Cloud (Firebase) servers in the United States
Perk usage, completions, cache Locally on your device
Authentication credentials Firebase Authentication (Google Cloud)

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in
  • Database security rules preventing unauthorized access
  • Users can only access their own data

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

Your data may only be disclosed:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights or safety
  • With your explicit consent

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Update

You can view and update your profile information at any time within the app's Profile screen.

7.2 Delete Your Data

You have the right to delete your account and all associated data at any time. See our Data Deletion Policy for details.

7.3 Guest Mode

You can use the app as a guest without providing any personal information. Guest data is stored only on your device.

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted
  • Subscriptions and votes are removed
  • Events you created are deleted (if no other subscribers) or marked as "[deleted]" creator
  • Local app data is cleared

9. Children's Privacy

Perks is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

10. International Users

If you are accessing Perks from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States where our servers are located.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date

Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
